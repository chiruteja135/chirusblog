Confessions

Dear earth, I (as part of we) humbly apologize for being insane ,exploitive creatures on earth.Below are the few things that hurt you most and I feel guilty for being utterly shameless in estimating the aftermath of the development activities which were initiated by us. I still remember that while i was studying in college on environment day in the name of celebration we were conducting seminar on effect of chloroflurocarbons sitting in Ac seminar hall(which is one of the major source of chloroflurocarbons production). It's like stabbing you with knife and saying we are trying to minimise the pain. One more thing is out of our greed (which we call as development and progress) we are constantly polluting the air and environment.Now to my little knowledge (it may be wrong) every household in India holds atleast one 2 wheeler emitting greenhouse gases. And the trend is going up every year.And again we come up with COP20 and SDG .(such a clever backup plan to show off).
