Tears

"Tears" one of the most valuable gifts every human being possess.(unfortunately most people don't recognise this). Like the Telugu saying "ఉప్పు కప్పురంబు నొక్క పోలిక నుండు చూడ చూడ రుచులు జాడ వేరు ఫురుషులందు ఫుణ్య పురుషులు వేరయ విశ్వధాభిరామ, వినుర వేమ" Essentially there are only two situations tears roll over one's cheeks. One is the case of extreme happiness/blissfullness (which happens on very very rare occasions and only in few people's lives). And the other is because of pain/sadness which most of the humanity is accustomed to right now I feel ,most of the Times this is not physical. Like said in the above Telugu poem, the chemical composition of tears in both the cases is same which essentially consists of water, electrolytes, proteins, lipids, and Mucins. So it's on us that for what reason we should stand in other's lives. Like the poet vemana said choose wisely...........
